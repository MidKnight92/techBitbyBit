# Privacy Policy for Tech Bit-by-Bit

**Last updated:** May 22, 2025

At **Tech Bit-by-Bit**, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic information, including:

- **Anonymous usage data** through tools like Umami (a self-hosted analytics platform)
- **Email addresses and profile data** if you sign up or log in using authentication (e.g., Clerk)
- **Information you provide voluntarily** through contact forms, comments, or subscriptions

We do **not** collect or store sensitive personal information such as government-issued IDs or payment details.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Improve the website and user experience
- Understand visitor behavior through anonymized analytics
- Communicate with subscribers who opt in to receive updates

## 3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

- Manage user sessions and authentication via Clerk
- Collect anonymous usage data through Umami
- Display personalized advertisements using **Google AdSense**

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Some features (like login) may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

## 4. Third-Party Links

This site includes links to third-party sites like YouTube, Buy Me a Coffee, and GitHub. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites or any third-party sites linked from techbitbybit.com.

## 5. Advertising

We may use services like **Google AdSense** to display ads. These third-party services may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can manage ad personalization through your [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).
